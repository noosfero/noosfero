h1(order-480). Removing comments

p.  _If you are the author of an article or if you have permission to moderate the comments of a profile (community/enterprise), you can remove the unwanted comments posted on it._

*  "How to access":#HowAccess
*  "Description":#Description
*  "Related Topics":#RelatedTopics

h2(#HowAccess). How to access

p. Visit an article that you created or that you have permission to moderate the comments. The comments are listed below the article/picture/event

h2(#Description). Description

# Next to each comment of the article, photo or event you will see a trash icon. To delete one, click on the trash icon.  !=/images/doc/comments.en.png(Comments)!
# You will be asked to confirm exclusion. Clickiing on "Ok" button, the comment will be removed  !=/images/doc/remove-comment-confirmation.en.png(Remove comment confirmation)!

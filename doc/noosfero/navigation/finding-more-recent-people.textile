h1(order-612). Finding more recent people

p.  _To find the people who joined the system in recent times._

*  "How to access":#HowAccess
*  "Description":#Description
*  "Related topics":#RelatedTopics

h2(#HowAccess). How to access

p. Find on top of page the term "People" and click on the arrow below it:
  !=/images/doc/menu-more-recent-people.en.png(More recent people on top menu)!

h2(#Description). Description
# The people will be listed ordered by creation date with most recent first. Below the profile's picture, you will see the date that the person joined the network !=/images/doc/more-recent-people-detail.en.png(Profile with creation date)!
# Fill in the search field with what you want to look for and click on "Search" !=/images/doc/more-recent-people.en.png(More recent people search field)!
# Then you will see the search results ordered by creation date !=/images/doc/more-recent-people-with-example.en.png(More recent people search results with example)!
# Clicking on one of the search results, you will be redirected to the homepage of choosen person.
